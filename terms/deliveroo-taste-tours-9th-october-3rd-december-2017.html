A hard copy of the T&Cs can be printed or a copy requested by post, by sending a stamped self-addressed envelope to the address at the end of these T&Cs.
  2. THE PRIZES:  The Promoter (defined below) is offering a chance to win one a trip each week for 8 consecutive weeks:

The trip will be be supplied by NDL Group. Details about the trip, and terms and the applicable terms and conditions are set out below.  

  1. HOW TO ENTER: Entry into this promotion each week is automatic, up until the point in time that the prize is awarded, for all Deliveroo customers who:
  1. meet the eligibility criteria set out in item 8 of these T&Cs; and
  2. order from any of the restaurants participating in the Taste Tour for that week ("Taste Tour Restaurants") through their Deliveroo account between 8am on the first day of the promotion and 11.59pm on the final day of the promotion (subject to the opening hours of each Taste Tour Restaurant).

Once the prize has been successfully claimed, the Promoter will post a confirmation on the Deliveroo Facebook page.  

  1. TASTE TOUR RESTAURANTS:  Taste Tour Restaurants for each week will be identified in promotional materials and tagged on the Deliveroo customer app and website with the wording "WIN A TASTE TOUR".  
  2. HOW TO WIN: The winning order for each week will contain a special Taste Tours passport document (Prize Passport) confirming that the recipient is the winner for that week.  The winning order will be determined by randomly selecting the Taste Tour Restaurant, date and time.  
  3. CLAIMING THE PRIZE: Once the winner has received a winning Passport, they will need to claim their prize within 7 days in accordance with the instructions set out on the Prize Passport.  If the prize has not been claimed within 7 days, it will be redrawn.    
  4. ELIGIBILITY: Promotion is open to all Irish customers aged 18 or over who are able to order through their Deliveroo account, excluding employees of the Promoter, employees of any Taste Tour Restaurants and any other person whom, in the Promoter's reasonable opinion, should be excluded due to their involvement or connection with this promotion.

The Promoter reserves the right to cancel or amend this Promotion due to events or circumstances arising beyond its control and, where necessary, to undertake all such action as is reasonable to protect itself against fraudulent or invalid redemptions including, without limitation, to require further verification as to the identity, age, and other relevant details of a customer.

Subject to item 11 of these terms, the Promoter will only use the personal details supplied in customer's accounts, and in connection with this promotion for the administration and delivery of an order, and the contact and verification of the prize winner and for no other purpose without first getting consent. The Promoter may disclose any customer's details personal information to its contractors or partners to assist in conducting this prize draw.
  7. By accepting any prize, the winner may be required to acknowledge that their name and image may be used for printing, broadcast, publicity and promotional purposes, including but not limited to the Promoter's websites and social media accounts without compensation.  The winner may also be asked to participate in publicity arising from the competition.  A winner will, if required by the Promoter, complete any documentation necessary to give effect to this assignment of right, title and interest.
  8. The Promoter may modify, suspend or terminate the promotion (e.g. extend or change the period during which it runs) or make any other changes to the promotion or these T&C.  Where reasonably possible, such changes will be notified in advance by posting amended T&Cs on this page and/or via the Promoters' social pages.
