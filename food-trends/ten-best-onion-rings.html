10 Tempting Ways To Feed Your Onion Ring Obsession

  • 21 June 2017
  • Dylan Newe
  • Read time: 4 min
onion rings sides snacks dublin galway limerick cork
Share this article

Occasionally overshadowed, endlessly underrated and deeply delicious...it's time to give onion rings the credit they're so duly deserved. Let's recognise them as being the ultimate side dish, ideal for any food scenario whether you're dining alone or ordering for many! We've rounded up some of the nation's best crispy and deep-fried onion rings. Luckily, we have all these available and ready to deliver straight to your door:

  1. Gourmet Burger Kitchen

A post shared by Sushant Javeri (@sushantjaveri) on Jun 13, 2017 at 6:19am PDT

Made from a secret GBK recipe and served with yummy garlic dip, this ridiculously tasty stack of deep-fried goodness will melt in your mouth. Devour them happily on their own or alongside one of GBK's famously jam-packed burgers or whopper milkshakes.

Where: Dublin (South Anne Street), Dublin (Temple Bar), Dublin (Swords) 

2. Jo'Burger

A post shared by Jo'Burger (@joburgerdublin) on Jun 15, 2017 at 2:28am PDT

Trying to decide between sides can be a tricky business but don't stress it! These super tasty beer-battered onion rings are the perfect complement to either one of Jo's mammoth burgers or with a host of other equally salivating bites.

Where: Dublin (Rathmines), Dublin (Smithfield)

3. Pitt Bros BBQ

The ultimate satisfying accompaniment to the classic Pitt Bros meat-feast favourites like pulled pork, brisket, chicken and ribs, the onion rings here are flawlessly crispy and ideally suited for sharing!

Where: Dublin (George's Street), Dublin (Millenium Walkway)

4. El Toro BBQ

A post shared by John McLaughlin (@johnmc88) on Oct 2, 2016 at 12:39pm PDT

Authentic deep south BBQ grub to die for, these heavenly beer-battered onion rings are the ideal foil for enjoying what can only be described as the carnivore's dream at this popular Limerick restaurant!

Where: Limerick 

5. Bobo's

A post shared by Valea_💟 (@valea_) on Mar 29, 2017 at 11:23am PDT

The superb onion rings here are just as stacked as the epic burgers which keep Dubliners very happy. Beer-battered and supremely crispy, these golden rings are served with homemade aioli and sweet chilli sauce.

Where: Dublin (Dame Street), Dublin (Wexford Street), Dublin (Abbey Street)

6. Rocket's

A post shared by Sophie Sharman (@s.c.sharman) on Mar 12, 2017 at 7:22am PDT

If you're looking for a substantial feed, you can't go wrong with Rocket's and their onion ring game is of course, equally strong. Large and immaculately battered, they're the glorious finish to any meal.

Where: Dublin (IFSC), Dublin (Baggot Street), Dublin (Dun Laoghaire), Cork (Douglas)

7. Super Miss Sue

A post shared by John Crowley (@flyguyjersey) on Jun 13, 2017 at 12:55pm PDT

Fish and chips may be the speciality of SMS Cervi with impeccable specials such as Haddock, Cod and House Scampi on the menu but they've also brought the same finesse to their succulent beer-battered onion rings.

Where: Dublin 

8. The Chili Shack

A post shared by Shane Fitzpatrick (@fitz_xoxo) on Mar 25, 2016 at 11:09am PDT

No-nonsense great food with sizeable portions, The Chili Shack never disappoints when it comes to solving your hunger fix. Make sure you add onion rings as the crowning glory to one of their nosh-tastic burgers!

Where: Dublin, Galway 

9. Beshoff Bros.

A post shared by Geoff Redknap (@geoffredknap) on Nov 26, 2016 at 2:14pm PST

Think gluten-free onion rings aren't as deliciously crunchy and crisply tender? Think again. Beshoff Bros. have been doing this for three generations so they've mastered the art of these crowd pleasers!

Where: Dublin (Mespil Road), Dublin (Dame Street)

10. The Washerwoman

A post shared by TheTaste.ie (@thetaste_ie) on May 8, 2017 at 12:50pm PDT

Because what's better than a well-cooked steak with some golden, crispy coated sides? The Washerwoman's Devil's Onion Rings are certainly worth the temptation whatever your main course is.

Where: Dublin
