College survival guide: student-friendly meal deals

  • 6 September 2017
  • Dylan Newe
  • Read time: 3 min
students meals deals dublin cork galway limerick
Share this article

Whether you're brand new to the college experience or have recently returned for another year of hard study, Deliveroo's got you covered with the best selection of tasty food. And since ordering in with a group is a great way to split costs and not break the bank while enjoying quality time with new flatmates and friends, here's just some of the best student-friendly meal deals available near you.

Double Up Deal, Aperitivo

A post shared by ollie shiel (@ollybajolly) on Jan 4, 2017 at 2:57am PST

Late nights out or even longer days spent in lectures are bound to build up a right appetite so what better way to fight the hunger than sharing some deliciously cheesy pizzas from Aperitivo.

What you get: 2 x 10 inch pizza and 2 drinks, €27

Where: Dublin (Temple Bar)

Meal Deal, Supermac's

A post shared by Hungry Girl (@hungrygirl_blog) on Aug 8, 2017 at 12:22am PDT

Between classes, assignments, societies and your all-important social life, you'll need some well-deserved downtime at points and nothing will be better than the comforting taste of one of Supermac's ace meal deals.

What you get: A 14" pizza, 3 fries and 3 drinks, €21.95

Where: Dublin (Temple Bar), Dublin (Talbot Street), Cork

Pizza Party, Da Vincenzo

A post shared by Caleb Purcell (@caleb_purcell_photo) on Dec 30, 2016 at 7:36am PST

Whether you're organising a classmate night in or just needing that elusive hangover cure, pizza is always the answer. Order from any of Da Vincenzo's signature pizzas and let Deliveroo take care of the rest.

What you get: Any 4 12" pizzas for €11 each

Where: Limerick

Paddy's Burger Meals, Paddy's Poutine

A post shared by Paddy's Poutine (@paddyspoutine) on May 11, 2017 at 9:41am PDT

Canada's national dish is Galway's gain and if it's some easy and delicious post-lecture grub you're after you can't go wrong with Paddy's Poutine. Choose from a host of toppings such as bacon, sausage and pudding.

What you get: Two Paddy's Poutines, Two Paddy's Burger Meals, 4 drinks, €29

Where: Galway 

Two Meat Combo, Smokin' Bones

A post shared by Marcelo Paolucci (@marcelopaolucci) on Jun 15, 2017 at 11:48am PDT

When you're juggling bookwork with an active social life and a part-time job, sometimes all you want is honest no-nonsense grub at the end of a long day. Go halves on a Smokin' Bones combo for a mouth-watering feed.

What you get: Any two meats/wings and any two sides, €16.95

Where: Dublin (Dame Street), Dublin (Drumcondra)

The Share Deal, Quattro Woodfire Pizza

A post shared by CheeseCentric (@cheesecentric) on Jul 21, 2017 at 5:16am PDT

After a long study day, you want to be able to sit back with an enjoyable pizza for two. The only thing for debate is whether you go for the spicy chorizo of the Diablo or the smoked mozzarella of the Formaggi.

What you get: Any "12 pizza and 2 drinks, €15

Where: Dublin

Deliveroo Special, Burritos and Blues

A post shared by 하나_Hana (@hana._choi) on Aug 12, 2017 at 4:31am PDT

Getting assignments handed in on time each time is a mini-triumph so when you do reward yourself with a packed, bulging with chicken and lots of salsa, beans, cheese and sour cream.

What you get: Grande chicken burrito, tortilla chips, bottle of water, €15

Where: Dublin (Wexford Street), Cork
