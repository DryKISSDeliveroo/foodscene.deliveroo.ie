Five creamy cheesecakes to sweep your cravings off their feet

  • 18 April 2018
  • Lucy Patterson
  • Read time: 3 min
dessert pudding
Share this article

The evening's meal is never truly complete until dessert is served and the sweet teeth have their turn. Whether you like your puddings fruity and light or with a chocolatey punch, Deliveroo has some of Ireland's best on offer, all at your convenience. Cheesecake: simultaneously tangy and sour, light and sweet, it's a civilised dish if ever there was one, with a thousand variations under the sun for connoisseurs to pursue. Cleanse your palate and try a handful - we've jotted down just five of our favourites for you to enjoy, each as colourful and delicious than the last. A biscuity base, a creamy mix, a new innovation on the theme. Bring on the list!

1. Oreo Cheesecake - The Briar Rose 

An Oreo crumble, an Oreo base, sweet Chantilly cream and drizzled chocolate sauce - the Oreo Cheesecake from The Briar Rose kitchen is luxury on a dainty white plate. You may think you're already familiar with this classic, American biscuit but think again! These guys really know how to take a snack and turn it into a world-class dessert. A family-run restaurant, theirs is as close to professional home cooking as take-out cheesecake can get, with the perfect balance of crunch and smooth tang in every forkful.

2. Baked Lemon and Raspberry Cheesecake - Zizzi 

To bake or not to bake? That is the eternal cheesecake question. At Zizzi, they opt for the latter without a moment's hesitation, taking Italian inspiration and heaping it into their dessert menu. If you're looking for a brighter, more fragrant aftertaste, their Baked Lemon and Raspberry Cheesecake is as fresh as can be. With a contrast of citrus and sweet, a vibrant mix of red and yellow, the smooth cream and the crunchy base, it'll whip your cravings into shape in no time at all. Great after a big meal, great after a small. You can't go wrong!

3. White Chocolate and Raspberry Cheesecake - Holy Smoke 

Holy Smoke are masters of the spice blend and kings of a red-hot barbecue. They know their meats, their racks and their burger buns inside out and upside down - but what cookout is complete without a little sweetness on the side? From the depths of their sizzling kitchen comes a cheesecake as delicate as it is delicious, studded with raspberry fruit and gleaming with white chocolate seduction. It's a classic combo, but these guys are turning the flavour up and notch and believe us - you should not miss out!

A post shared by flavour.ie (@flavour.ie) on Mar 21, 2017 at 5:13am PDT

4. White Chocolate Cheesecake - Cafe Bliss

Specialising in fresh, wholesome foods, Cafe Bliss are all about the business of culinary pleasure. Creators of the final entry on our cheesecake countdown, they treat their customers well. An order of their White Chocolate Cheesecake is an order of satisfaction. You can rest assured that a bite of this beauty is balanced to a tee. Not too sweet, not too subtle. Just right.

Indulge, enjoy - from creamed cheesecake to fruity bites, order dessert directly to your door with Deliveroo.
