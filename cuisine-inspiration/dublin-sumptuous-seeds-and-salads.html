Sumptuous Seeds And Salads – Have A Healthy Body In 2018

  • 18 January 2018
  • Sam Cunningham
  • Read time: 3 min
dublin seeds salads chickpeas hummus
Share this article

These days it's easy and enjoyable to eat food that's good for your body with the numerous recent innovations in the realms of healthy eating. For those who are passionate about injecting their bodies with a considerable dose of goodness, there are some seriously sumptuous seeds and salads available on restaurant menus in Dublin. You should strongly consider going meat-free, and check out the bustling vegetarian scene in the city. Here we have listed some of the best options for you to try.

1. Hummus

A post shared by lizi (@lizipooo) on Sep 28, 2016 at 12:33pm PDT

Hummus is one of the best-known and most popular Mediterranean foods and enjoying a feast of delights from the region wouldn't be the same without it. Keshk is a unique restaurant which specialises in North African, Greek, Turkish and vegetarian dishes which are absolutely bursting with flavour. You'll want to order plenty of things from the menu here, but make sure you accompany them with a massive portion of their wholesome and heavenly hummus.

2. Toasted Seed Mix

Staple Foods is a gluten-free vegetarian breakfast joint which is home to a number of fabulous dishes that will help you keep up with your new healthy eating regime in scrumptious style. While there are loads of luscious pancakes and sourdough toasts on offer, we've singled out the toasted seed mix as a natural, nourishing snack to see you through the long days of January. While in the past you may have reached for a packet of crisps or a chocolate bar between meals, munching on nutritious seeds will fill you up and make you feel good at the same time.

3. Chickpea Salad

A post shared by Dine in Dublin (@dineindublin) on Jan 29, 2016 at 2:58am PST

This may not look like the most Instagrammable meal you've ever had, but trust us when we say that it is packed full of yumminess and things that are great for your body. Blazing Salads on Drury Street is the number one place for buying incredibly large salads in Dublin and is ideal for parties, work functions, or other special occasions. All of their salads are made to serve ten people and are reasonably priced at €64 each. Of all the extremely healthy options on the menu here, we think that the Chickpea Salad is a cut above the rest. It's bursting with vitamins and nutrients, and, with its high fibre content, will have you feeling full for the rest of the day.

4. Large Salad

A post shared by The Good Food Store (@the_good_food_store) on Jul 5, 2016 at 1:29am PDT

The Good Food Store has only been open since 2015, but it has already made a name for itself as one of the best places in Dublin for providing healthy, tasty food. It's a deli which specialises in Irish and European-style wraps and sandwiches as well as providing products for other restaurants. The store always has fresh and succulent salads on offer that are totally customisable as customers can choose the ingredients they want to be included. We recommend going for the large option so you can get all those extra vital vitamins and nutrients.

Are you ready to treat your body with the respect it deserves in 2018? Order these incredibly healthy dishes and more, straight to your door with Deliveroo.
