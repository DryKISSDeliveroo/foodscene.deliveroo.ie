Around The World In Five Meals... And All Delivered To Your Door In Dundrum!

  • 21 March 2018
  • Lucy Patterson
  • Read time: 3 min
indian thai italian mexican japanese pasta
Share this article

Let's face it. We all want to travel but, as rich as the experiences may be, a lot of us are rather confined to our sofas, be it on account of a busy lifestyle or the expense involved. There's no need to panic, though, as you can embark on culinary adventures from the comfort of your very own home. Whether you want Indian takeaway or something with a little less of a kick, our research shows that you Dundrum lot are a diverse bunch. You really love food from all over the world, so here are a few of our fave dishes from some of the farthest flung corners – all of the taste, none of the expensive flights.

1. Italian – Penne Carbonara

Italy is known for its incredible wealth of rich and tasty food. It's really hard to narrow down their finest specialities to just one option when you have an abundance of pizza and pasta to choose from. But we have and the Penne Carbonara is right up there with the best. Jamie's Italian make an amazing one right here in Ireland, featuring sweet tender leeks with smoked pancetta, finished off with cracked black pepper and parmesan. If you close your eyes you can almost imagine you're sitting in Rome.

A post shared by Jamie's Italian Ireland (@jamiesitalianire) on Dec 15, 2015 at 4:56am PST

2. Indian – Tikka Masala

Tikka Masala is arguably one of the most famous dishes to featured on Indian menus. Its base is a creamy sauce, made all the more rich by a healthy helping of tomatoes. Its orange colouring is instantly recognisable – but the taste is its most unique feature. There are many different variations but at Chutney Mary they add chicken, fenugreek and cardamon.

3. Thai – Thai Stir-Fried Beef

Thai food is delicious. Whether it's creamy and coconut-based or flavourful and fragrant – or a combination of them all – it won't let you down. Thai Stir Fried Beef from My Thai really highlights some of the best parts of the cuisine, with marinated meats, veggies and noodles. The restaurant really pays homage to local produce, using Irish fillet steak strips to work in perfect harmony with Thai chillies and rice wine as well as oyster and soy sauce.

4. Mexican – Burrito

Tacos, nachos, fajitas... all staples of Mexican cuisine. But it's burritos we're going mad for and they are at their best when ordered from Zambrero. 12-inch tortillas packed full of pinto beans, rice, meat and a whole host of extras. Salsa really spices up the whole affair while cheese brings the dish back down to earth. Delicious.

A post shared by Zambrero Ireland (@zambreroireland) on Nov 13, 2017 at 2:43pm PST

5. Japanese – Salmon Sashimi

The Japanese are known for their incredibly fresh fish and sashimi is the ultimate raw delicacy. Hailan Restaurant uses the highest-grade salmon in their salmon sashimi and the taste is astounding. It's simple – it's just raw, sliced fish – but there's nothing fresher. You're going to love both it and the sensation of worldliness that comes with it!

A post shared by Twisted Eggs (@twistedeggs) on May 27, 2016 at 3:36pm PDT

You don't have to go far to enjoy some of the world's finest dishes. In fact, order them right now from wherever you're sitting on Deliveroo.
